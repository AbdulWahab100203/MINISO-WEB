About Us - Miniso

Miniso is a Japanese-inspired brand offering a variety of high-quality, affordable lifestyle products.

Our Story

Founded in 2013, Miniso quickly became a global phenomenon by bringing a unique blend of innovation, quality, and affordability to lifestyle products. Our goal is to make everyday essentials accessible to everyone.

Click the button below to learn more about Miniso's history!

Miniso History

Miniso was founded with the aim of providing high-quality products at reasonable prices. Our journey has been one of constant growth, expanding our reach to over 80 countries worldwide.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To deliver joy through affordable and high-quality products.

Vision: To become a household name recognized for quality lifestyle products around the world.

Core Values

Quality

We maintain the highest standards for all our products.

Affordability

We believe quality products should be accessible to everyone.

Innovation

We continuously innovate to improve our product offerings.
